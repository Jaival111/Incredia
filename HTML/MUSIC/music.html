Welcome to India’s Musical Heritage

Discover the Soul of Indian Music

India’s musical culture is a harmonious blend of tradition and innovation, reflecting the rich history and diverse heritage of this ancient land. From the classical rhythms of the Vedas to the contemporary beats of Bollywood, Indian music encompasses a wide array of genres, styles, and forms. Whether you are a connoisseur of classical music, a fan of folk tunes, or an enthusiast of modern fusion, there is something for everyone in India’s vibrant musical tapestry.

Explore Our Musical Treasures

Classical Music

Delve into the timeless world of Indian classical music, where every note tells a story of centuries-old traditions and masterful artistry.

Hindustani Classical
Experience the grandeur of North Indian classical music with maestros like Ravi Shankar and Zakir Hussain.

Carnatic Classical
Immerse yourself in the intricate melodies of South Indian classical music with legends like M.S. Subbulakshmi and L. Subramaniam.

Folk Music

Journey through the heartlands of India with our diverse folk music traditions, each echoing the unique cultural essence of its region.

Punjab’s Bhangra
Feel the energy of Punjab with the vibrant beats of Bhangra and Giddha.

Gujarat’s Garba
Dance to the rhythmic circles of Garba and Dandiya during festive seasons.

Maharashtra’s Lavani
Enjoy the storytelling dance and music of Lavani.

Popular Music

From Bollywood to Indie bands, explore the sounds that define modern Indian music.

Bollywood Hits
Sing along to the latest chartbusters and evergreen classics from Bollywood.

Indie and Fusion
Discover the new wave of Indian music blending traditional and global influences with artists like Indian Ocean and Nucleya.

Religious and Devotional Music

Experience the spiritual side of Indian music through devotional songs and hymns that uplift the soul.

Bhajans
Listen to the soulful praises sung in Hindu temples.

Qawwalis
Feel the mystical aura of Sufi devotional music.

Kirtans
Join the melodic worship in Sikh Gurdwaras.

Music & Dance

Music Festivals

India is home to numerous music festivals that celebrate its rich heritage and contemporary talent. Don’t miss out on these musical extravaganzas:

Classical Music Festivals
Tyagaraja Aradhana, Sawai Gandharva Bhimsen Festival.

Modern Music Festivals
Sunburn Festival, NH7 Weekender.

Dance

Explore the inseparable bond between dance and music in India. Learn about classical dance forms like Bharatanatyam and Kathak, and enjoy folk dances such as Bhangra and Garba, each with its unique musical accompaniment.
